Iced Pumpkin Spice Latte

Description

This iced pumpkin spice latte tastes just like a coffee shop latte, and takes hardly any time to throw together—and it’s super customizable. You can use whatever kind of milk you prefer; and increase or decrease the amount of espresso, maple syrup, pumpkin, or spice to suit your tastes.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot brewed espresso
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons pumpkin puree, or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice, or to taste
  • 1 pinch salt
  • ice as needed
  • whipped cream (optional)

Steps

  1. Place espresso, milk, maple syrup, pumpkin, vanilla, pumpkin pie spice, and salt into the cup of a high-speed blender. Blend on High speed until fully smooth and combined, about 30 seconds.
  2. Fill a glass with ice. Pour pumpkin mixture over ice and serve cold. If desired, serve topped with whipped cream and an extra sprinkle of pumpkin pie spice.

Enjoy!
